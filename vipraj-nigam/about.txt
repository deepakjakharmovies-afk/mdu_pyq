A talented leg-spinner from Uttar Pradesh, Vipraj Nigam has proven to be a reliable bowler in domestic cricket, especially in middle-over containment. His performances caught the attention of Delhi Capitals, who signed him for INR 50 Lakh at the 2025 TATA IPL auction.
