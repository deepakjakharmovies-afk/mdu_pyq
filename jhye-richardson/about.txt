Aussie bowlers and their obsession with pace is a match made in heaven as young right arm pacer Jhye Richardson made it to the club, consistently bowling at decent speeds as well as maintaining accuracy at the same time. Richardson had rapid career progress after finding success in both Sheffield Shield as well as the BBL. He was the fourth most expensive player sold in the 2021 IPL auction after the Punjab Kings acquired him for a whopping price of INR 14 cr. It would be a significant achievement for the right arm pacer if he manages to translate his past success to the IPL.
