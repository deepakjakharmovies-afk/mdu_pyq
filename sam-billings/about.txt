Delhi Capitals bought Sam Billings for INR 2 Cr during the player auction ahead of the IPL 2021. The wicketkeeper-batsman has represented England in 52 games – 22 ODIs and 30 T20Is – and has so far played 187 T20s and has over 3500 runs to his name. Billings has played 22 IPL games across four seasons and will be looking to make it count for Delhi Capitals.
