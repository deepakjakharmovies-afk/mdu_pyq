Abishek Porel stepped up as Rishabh Pant’s replacement in IPL 2023 and has since played 18 matches across two seasons, scoring 360 runs at a strike rate of over 150. Recognizing his potential, Delhi Capitals retained him as an uncapped player ahead of the TATA IPL 2025 Auction. The young wicketkeeper-batter will continue to be a crucial part of their squad.
