M. Siddharth has been a mainstay in the Tamil Nadu domestic side in the recent few years. The left-arm spinner has been pretty economical in the domestic circuit and has been a part of KKR and DC setups in IPL. He moved to LSG in IPL 2024, where he got the wicket of Virat Kohli as his maiden IPL scalp. Siddharth can drift the ball in the air and extract turn away from the right-handers. LSG showed their trust in him and secured him once again for TATA IPL 2025 at the price of INR 75 Lakh.
