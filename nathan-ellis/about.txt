Australian speedster Nathan Ellis has made a name for himself as a specialist in T20 cricket with his variations and ability to bowl under pressure. A smart operator, Ellis is a wicket-taker who can bowl in different phases of the game. After four seasons with Punjab Kings, Ellis was snapped up by CSK for INR 2 Crore at the 2025 TATA IPL Auction.
