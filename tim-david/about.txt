The tall and powerful middle-order batter earned a reputation as one of the most sought-after T20 finishers in the world with his impactful batting in a range of tournaments. His first stint in the TATA IPL was with Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB) in 2021, but he grabbed great attention when Mumbai Indians spent INR 8.25 crore to bring him on board in the 2022 Mega Auction. He showed why he was rated so highly in the first season itself, boasting a strike rate of more than 200, including a match-winning 46 off 18 against Sunrisers Hyderabad which was his most prominent knock from that campaign. David has been a regular feature in the Australian T20I set-up over the past few years. Now back with RCB ahead of IPL 2025, the Bengaluru-based team will bank on him to provide that crucial impetus in the slog overs.
