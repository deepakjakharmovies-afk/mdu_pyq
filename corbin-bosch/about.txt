Born in Durban, South Africa, all-rounder Corbin Bosch was earlier a part of Rajasthan Royals squad as a net bowler before the 27-year old replaced the injured Nathan Coulter-Nile at the price of INR 20 Lakh. With over 150 runs and 18 wickets in 30 T20s to his name, the all-rounder can prove to be valuable with both bat and ball and further strengthen the Rajasthan Royals squad with his overall contribution.
