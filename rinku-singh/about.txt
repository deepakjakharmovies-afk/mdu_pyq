From being picked by KKR for INR 80 lakh at the 2018 IPL auction to being retained for Rs 13 crore ahead of IPL 2025, Rinku Singh has come a long way. Rinku gained the spotlight when he hit five sixes in the last over of an IPL chase, becoming the only batter to do so and thereby earning a national team call-up. He will play a key role as KKR look to defend their IPL title.
