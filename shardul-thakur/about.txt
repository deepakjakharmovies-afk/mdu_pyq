A proper utility and impact player, Shardul Thakur is a right-arm pacer who has the ability to hit big and deliver in crunch situations. A hit the deck bowler, Shardul Thakur swings the ball away from the right-handers and also has a great split finger slower ball in his arsenal. Shardul has produced vital performances for Mumbai in the domestic circuit and has also been a part of the IPL since 2015. He played a crucial role, with 16 wickets, in CSK’s title-winning run in 2018. The all-rounder has also put up impressive performances for India across all three formats of the game. Shardul Thakur will replace an injured Mohsin Khan in the Lucknow Super Giants squad for TATA IPL 2025.
