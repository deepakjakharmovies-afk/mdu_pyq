A rising right-arm off-spinner from Andhra Pradesh, Tripurana Vijay came to the limelight in Ranji Trophy 2024/25, registering a brilliant 10-wicket match haul against Rajasthan. His ability to bowl economically in pressure situations has made him a valuable asset in domestic cricket. Delhi Capitals invested INR 30 Lakh to bring the young spinner into their squad for TATA IPL 2025.
