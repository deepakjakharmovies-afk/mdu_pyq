One of India’s talented pool of left-arm spin bowling all-rounders, Krunal Pandya is equally effective in all three departments of the game. Along with his range of skills, the older of the Pandya brothers also brings championship-winning expertise as he has been a part of three title-winning sides at Mumbai Indians (MI). Pandya is known for his ability to contain opposition batters while also picking wickets, as well as striking at a brisk pace as a batter in the middle order. After his highly successful time at MI, Pandya was also part of the Lucknow Super Giants (LSG) side that made back-to-back playoff appearances in 2022 and 2023. Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB) acquired his services for TATA IPL 2025 at a price of INR 5.75 Crore.
