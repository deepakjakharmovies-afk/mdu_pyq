Ramakrishna Ghosh, a pace-bowling all-rounder from Maharashtra, has shown glimpses of brilliance in the domestic circuit. Having previously played under CSK captain Ruturaj Gaikwad at the domestic level, Ghosh will look to make an impact if given an opportunity in TATA IPL 2025. He earned a spot in the CSK squad for INR 30 Lakh.
