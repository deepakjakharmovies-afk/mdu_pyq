An all-rounder hailing from Punjab, Ramandeep Singh made his IPL debut in 2022 after being picked up by Mumbai Indians. However, he gained recognition during IPL 2024, where he came in handy for the Knight Riders as they clinched the trophy. Ramandeep was retained by KKR ahead of the IPL 2025 mega auction.
