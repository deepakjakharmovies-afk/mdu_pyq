One of only two Indians to have scored a Test triple-century, Karun Nair has been a dominant force in domestic cricket. The Karnataka-born batter, now representing Vidarbha, had a stellar 2024/25 season, amassing 779 runs in the Vijay Hazare Trophy at an average of 389.50, and 863 runs in the Ranji Trophy, leading Vidarbha to the title. He even smashed 255 runs at a strike rate of over 170 in the Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy 2024/25. His recent resurgence saw him rejoin Delhi Capitals for INR 50 Lakh at the 2025 TATA IPL Auction.
