Kamindu Mendis is an exciting Sri Lankan all-rounder known for his ambidextrous bowling ability, capable of switching between right-arm off-spin and left-arm orthodox spin. This rare skill makes him a valuable asset, especially in T20 cricket, where captains can use him strategically against both right-handed and left-handed batters. Mendis first rose to prominence for his bowling versatility but quickly gained attention for his impressive batting performances at the international level. The young left-hander made waves by matching Don Bradman's record of reaching 1000 Test runs in just 13 innings. For TATA IPL 2025, Sunrisers Hyderabad secured Mendis for INR 75 Lakh.
