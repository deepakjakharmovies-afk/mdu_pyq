A flamboyant right-hand top-order batsman, Manish Pandey is among the very handful/few players to have played in every edition of the IPL since its inception. He also holds the distinction of being the first Indian to score a ton in the IPL. The 35-year old was roped in by Kolkata Knight Riders for INR 75 lakh.
