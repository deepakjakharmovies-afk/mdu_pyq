Chris Woakes did not feature in IPL 2020 but Delhi Capitals retained the England cricketer for the IPL 2021. This will be Woakes’ third IPL season, who has so far played 18 games in the tournament across two seasons and has 25 wickets to his name. The right-arm pacer, who is also a handy batsman, will look forward to enjoy a fine season with the Delhi franchise.
