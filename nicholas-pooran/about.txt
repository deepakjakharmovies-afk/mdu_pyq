A hard-hitting left-handed wicketkeeper batter, Nicholas Pooran has established himself as one of the cleanest strikers of the cricket ball. Known for his fast and swift bat swing, Pooran strikes close to 150 in T20 cricket. Time and time again, Pooran has played match-winning knocks for the West Indies. He has been a part of RCB, PBKS, SRH, and LSG setups in the IPL. His best season came in 2024 for LSG, where he racked up 499 runs at an average of 62.37 and a strike rate of 178.31. He was thus retained by the franchise for TATA IPL 2025.
