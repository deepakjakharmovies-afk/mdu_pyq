The cricket world took notice of Devdutt Padikkal's talent when he won the TATA IPL Emerging Player award in 2020 with a tally of 473 runs for RCB. He backed it up with another 400-run plus season, including a superb knock of 101* off just 52 deliveries against Rajasthan Royals. A solid left-handed batter who excels at the top of the order, Padikkal is known for the range of shots in his arsenal. He has represented India in Test cricket. After plying his trade with RR and LSG over the last couple of seasons, he is back with RCB for TATA IPL 2025.
