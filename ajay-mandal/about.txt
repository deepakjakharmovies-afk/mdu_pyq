A seasoned left-handed all-rounder, Ajay Mandal holds the record for being Chhattisgarh’s all-time leading wicket-taker in first-class cricket. He was a part of the Chennai Super Kings squad in IPL 2023 and 2024. Delhi Capitals have shown faith in his abilities by acquiring him at his base price of INR 30 Lakh for TATA IPL 2025.
