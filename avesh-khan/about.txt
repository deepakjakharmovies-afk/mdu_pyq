Avesh Khan is a tall, right-arm pace bowler from Madhya Pradesh who first came to the limelight at the 2016 Under-19 Cricket World Cup where he finished as India’s highest wicket-taker. Avhesh Khan has represented RCB, DC, LSG and RR in the past and will be back in Blue & Orange having been picked for 9.75 Crore.
