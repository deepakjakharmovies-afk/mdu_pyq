Narine has been one of the mainstays of the Knight Riders unit having joined them in 2012. The Trinidad-born spinner has established himself as an exceptional spin bowler with the ability to bamboozle batsmen, but over the years has also contributed when required with the bat making him a lethal weapon to have. He was once again retained by the franchise for IPL 2025.
