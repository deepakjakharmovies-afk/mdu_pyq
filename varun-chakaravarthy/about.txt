Varun Chakaravarthy has been instrumental to KKR's fortunes since having joined them in 2020. In the very first year, the mystery spinner made an impact picking 17 wickets in 13 matches and over the years has been displaying consistent performance. In IPL 2024, Varun picked up 21 wickets as KKR clinched their third title.
