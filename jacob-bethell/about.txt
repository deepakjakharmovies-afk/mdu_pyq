A left-handed batting all-rounder who is quite handy with his slow left-arm bowling and electric fielding, Jacob Bethell is capable of winning matches on his own. Bethell was impressive during England's 2022 U-19 World Cup campaign and boosted by more consistent performances in domestic cricket, he made his international debut for England in 2024 at the age of 20. Having represented England across formats and being a key part of their limited-overs set-up, Bethell is set for his TATA IPL debut with RCB.
