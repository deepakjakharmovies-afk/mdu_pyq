The right arm pacer picked five wickets in the seven games he played for the Mumbai Indians in the 2020 season. MI bought him again in the 2021 auctions for INR 5 cr. The fast bowler has been part of the IPL since 2013; he represented the Mumbai Indians in his first year in the league, was Delhi Daredevils’ joint-highest wicket-taker in the 2015 season, and donned the Kolkata Knight Riders jersey in 2017. At the IPL Player Auction 2018, RCB snapped him for INR 2.20 cr. One of the most promising prospects to emerge from Western Australia in recent years, Coulter-Nile can test batsmen with pace and the steep bounce he can generate bounce off a good length. The tall speedster also possesses the ability to hit big and can contribute crucial runs down the order.
