Standing tall at 6'3', Gurjapneet Singh is a left-arm medium pacer who originally hails from Punjab but moved to Chennai at 17 and has been a net bowler for CSK and the Indian Cricket Team. His ability to extract movement with the new ball and generate extra bounce makes him a valuable asset. CSK secured him for INR 2.2 Crore at the 2025 TATA IPL Auction.
