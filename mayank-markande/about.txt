Mayank Markande is a traditional right-arm leg-break bowler who is known to have a deceptive slower delivery in his arsenal. Markande has picked up 37 wickets in 37 games and will be looking to add when he dons the purple this year. One of his standout performances came during his debut when he picked up a 3-wicket haul against CSK in 2018.
