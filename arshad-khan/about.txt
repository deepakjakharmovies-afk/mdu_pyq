A left-arm pacer who can also deliver powerful lower-order cameos, Mohammad Arshad Khan made his IPL debut in 2023 after being picked up by Mumbai Indians in 2022. He later moved to Lucknow Super Giants in IPL 2024, where he made an impression with an unbeaten 58 against Delhi Capitals. His ability to contribute with both bat and ball made him an exciting prospect, leading Gujarat Titans to sign him for INR 1.30 Crore ahead of TATA IPL 2025.
