Wicketkeeper batter Urvil Patel joined the Chennai Super Kings squad as a replacement for Vansh Bedi in TATA IPL 2025. Urvil plays for Gujarat in the domestic circuit and is known for his hard-hitting abilities. He has been a part of the Gujarat Titans setup earlier in the IPL. He enjoyed a great domestic season in 2024/25 which included a record-breaking 28-ball century against Tripura in the Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy. This was the second-fastest hundred in T20 cricket and the fastest by an Indian.
