A technically sound top-order batsman, Aiden Markram made way into the IPL during the 2021 season having been picked up by Punjab Kings. In the subsequent year he moved to Sunrisers Hyderabad where he spent three seasons, including captaining them for a season. The player will now turn out for the Lucknow based franchise after being picked for his base price of INR 2 Crore.
