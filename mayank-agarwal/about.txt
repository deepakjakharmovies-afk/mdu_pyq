After skipper KL Rahul, Mayank Agarwal remains the most-important Indian batsman in the PBKS line-up. Mayank has enjoyed a fine run with the Indian Test team. Mayank can strike the ball hard and long and will be responsible for helping PBKS get big totals. He scored 2 half-centuries in 2019 and aggregated 332 runs at an average of 25.53. He had an eventful 2020 season where he scored a spectacular ton against the Rajasthan Royals and made an impactful performance count in the iconic double super-over game against the Mumbai Indians. Mayank is an experienced contender to play a vital role for the Punjab Kings’ success in 2021.
