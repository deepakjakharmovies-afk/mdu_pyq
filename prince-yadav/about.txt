Prince Yadav first attracted eyeballs with his performance at a local tournament in Delhi, where he showed the ability to bowl good yorkers in the slog overs. He followed up that performance by picking 11 wickets in the Vijay Hazare Trophy, finishing as Delhi’s highest wicket-taker. LSG picked the pacer at the IPL 2025 auction for INR 30 Lakh. It will be a great opportunity for him to play alongside some of the best in the world and make an impact.
