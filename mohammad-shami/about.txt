Mohammad Shami, one of India's most prolific pace bowlers, has made a name for himself with his remarkable ability to move the ball both ways and his immaculate seam position. With over 450 international wickets and 100+ IPL wickets, Shami has been a mainstay in the league since his debut in 2013, playing for KKR, DC, PBKS, and GT. In the IPL 2022, Shami played a pivotal role in Gujarat Titans' title-winning campaign, finishing with 20 wickets. He followed that up with an even more impressive performance in IPL 2023, claiming 28 wickets and winning the coveted Purple Cap. Sunrisers Hyderabad secured him for a significant INR 10 Crore at the 2025 TATA IPL Auction, making him their second-highest buy.
