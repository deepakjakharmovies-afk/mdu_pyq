A fearless T20 batter, Tristan Stubbs made his IPL debut in 2022 for Mumbai Indians, even before making his international debut for South Africa. However, it was with Delhi Capitals in IPL 2024 that he truly announced himself, finishing as the team’s second-highest run-getter with 378 runs at an average of 54 and a strike rate of 190.90. His match-winning performances earned him a retention for TATA IPL 2025.
