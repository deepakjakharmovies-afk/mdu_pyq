A rare left-arm wrist spinner, Kuldeep Yadav is known for his deceptive variations and lethal googlies. He was instrumental in India’s triumphs at the T20 World Cup 2024 and Champions Trophy 2025. After spending five seasons with KKR (2016-2020), he found a new home in Delhi Capitals in 2022, delivering his best IPL season with 21 wickets. His consistent performances over the last three seasons earned him a retention ahead of TATA IPL 2025.
