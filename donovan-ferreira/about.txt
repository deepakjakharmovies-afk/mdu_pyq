South African all-rounder Donovan Ferreira was a part of Rajasthan Royals setup in IPL 2023 and 2024. Ferreira is a seasoned T20 cricketer known for his explosive hitting and handy off-spin. He will provide valuable balance to the Delhi Capitals squad after being signed for INR 75 Lakh at the 2025 TATA IPL Auction.
