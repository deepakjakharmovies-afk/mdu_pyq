West Indies all-rounder Sherfane Rutherford is a left-handed middle-order batter and right-arm medium-fast bowler. Known for his quick-fire cameos and aggressive batting, Rutherford has been a key figure in various domestic competitions and has featured in 40 international white-ball matches, including T20Is, where he strikes at over 140. Having previously represented DC, MI, SRH, KKR, and RCB, Rutherford was acquired by Gujarat Titans for INR 2.6 Crore at the 2025 TATA IPL Auction. His explosive batting and versatility will further strengthen the Titans' middle order.
