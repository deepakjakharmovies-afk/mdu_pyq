Hailing from Delhi, Harshit Rana came into the IPL scene as a replacement for injured Rasikh Salam during the 2022 season and has slowly established himself as one of the mainstays of KKR's pace bowling department. The pacer on the back of an impressive IPL 2024 season was retained by Knight Riders.
