Only the fourth cricketer from Goa to have landed an IPL contract, the 23-year old Suyash Prabhudessai is known for his ability to score quick runs later down the batting order. At the IPL 2021 Player Auction, Suyash was picked by the RCB and the youngster is all set to play bold this season.
