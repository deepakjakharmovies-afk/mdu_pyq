Those who have seen Anmolpreet bat have predicted a bright future for this batsman from Punjab. He first played for India U19 in the 2015 World Cup and has continued to impress. Since then, he has graduated to India A level and played a crucial hand in India A team’s triumph against New Zealand A. Mumbai Indians got him early for INR 80 lakh in the 2019 season and he has managed to maintain his spot in the MI squad, entering confidently into the 14th edition.
