Shubham Dubey, a hard-hitting left-handed batter from Vidarbha, grabbed attention with a blistering 58 off 20 balls in the 2023 Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy, boasting a stunning strike rate of 290. Playing as a finisher, he amassed 221 runs at an average of 73.66 and a strike rate of 187.28, emerging as Vidarbha’s second-highest run-scorer in their quarter-final run. Continuing his form in SMAT 2024-25, Dubey smashed 134 runs at an average of 67 and a strike rate of 181.08, including a quickfire 30 off 14 against Odisha. After being part of Rajasthan Royals' set-up in IPL 2024, he will don the Pink jersey again in 2025, having been picked for INR 80 lakh. Known for his explosive finishing, Dubey will aim to make a significant impact in the slog overs.
