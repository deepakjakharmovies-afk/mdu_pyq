Sri Lankan speedster Dushmantha Chameera is known for his ability to strike with the new ball and deliver crucial overs in the slog overs. The tall pacer has vast T20 experience, along with over 100 international appearances across all formats for Sri Lanka. After stints with Rajasthan Royals (2018) and RCB (2021), he got a full-fledged IPL season with Lucknow Super Giants in 2022, claiming nine wickets in 12 matches. He was later a part of KKR’s title-winning squad in IPL 2024. Delhi Capitals secured his services for INR 75 Lakh at the IPL 2025 auction.
