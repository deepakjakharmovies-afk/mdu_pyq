Nagaland left-arm seamer Akash Singh was part of Rajasthan Royals, Chennai Super Kings and Sunrisers Hyderabad in the previous editions and was also a part of India’s Under-19 World Cup team in 2020. He will now play for Lucknow in IPL 2025 after being picked for INR 30 Lakh.
