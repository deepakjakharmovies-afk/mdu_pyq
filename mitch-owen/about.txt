Mitchell Owen is an Australian all-rounder known for his power-hitting abilities top of the order. Owen strikes over 180 in T20 cricket with two centuries to his name. He can clear the ropes with ease and he comes in as a replacement player for Glenn Maxwell in the Punjab Kings squad for TATA IPL 2025. Mitchell Owen will join PBKS for INR 3 Crore.
