A powerful left-handed hitter, Shivam Dube has been a revelation since joining CSK in 2022. His explosive batting helped CSK win IPL 2023, where he scored 418 runs at a strike rate of 158.33. Dube also played a key role in India’s T20 World Cup 2024 triumph. He has the knack of picking up wickets at crucial junctures with his handy medium-pacers. He was retained ahead of TATA IPL 2025 and will again be a vital cog for the franchise heading into the new season.
