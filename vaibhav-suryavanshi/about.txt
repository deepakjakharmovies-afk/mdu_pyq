Vaibhav Suryavanshi, a rising talent from Bihar, made history in 2024 by debuting in the Ranji Trophy at just 12, becoming the second youngest to do so for Bihar. Known for his elegant stroke play, he set a record with a 58-ball century against Australia U-19’s, the fastest by an Indian in youth Tests. His rapid rise saw him become the youngest player to earn an IPL contract, with Rajasthan Royals securing him for INR 1.1 crore ahead of IPL 2025. Training alongside stars like Sanju Samson and Yashasvi Jaiswal, he now has the perfect platform to refine his game and shape a bright future.
