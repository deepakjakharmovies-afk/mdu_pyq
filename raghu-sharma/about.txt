Raghu Sharma is a leg spinner from Punjab who joined Mumbai Indians as a replacement for Vignesh Puthur at his base price of INR 30 lakhs from the RAPP list. He was a part of the MI's support bowlers group earlier and has represented Punjab across the three formats in the domestic circuit. The leggie had a decent outing in the Vijay Hazare Trophy 2024/25 season with 14 wickets in eight matches.
