Known for his hard-hitting abilities, Abdul Samad made his IPL debut in 2020 after being picked by SRH, which made him only the fourth cricketer from Jammu and Kashmir after Parvez Rasool, Manzoor Dar & Rasikh Salam to earn an IPL contract. Over the years he became a regular feature in the team scoring some crucial runs in the middle-order. Ahead of IPL 2025, the J&K batter was picked by Lucknow for INR 4.2 Crore.
