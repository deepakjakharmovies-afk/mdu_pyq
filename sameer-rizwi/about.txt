A young lower-order power-hitter, Sameer Rizvi gained attention for his aggressive approach against spin. He made a mark in IPL 2024 when he slog-swept Rashid Khan for a six off his very first ball in the league while playing for CSK. Delhi Capitals secured him for INR 95 Lakh at the 2025 TATA IPL Auction to strengthen their batting depth.
