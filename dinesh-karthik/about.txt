Dinesh Karthik is one of the most experienced wicketkeeper-batsmen in the scheme of things. He led KKR to playoffs in 2018 and his side finished fifth in the points table in 2019. Post the Nidahas Trophy in Sri Lanka in 2018, Karthik has upped the ante in terms of his finishing skills in the shortest version. An IPL career tally of 3823 runs at a strike rate of 129.63 plus being tidy behind the stumps is testimony to the value he brings to the table.
