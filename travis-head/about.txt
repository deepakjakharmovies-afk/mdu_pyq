Australia’s left-hander Travis Head is one of the most aggressive batters in world cricket and can even bowl handy off-spin. Head is known for stepping up on big occasions as reflected by his match-winning centuries in the World Test Championship 2021-23 final and ODI World Cup 2023 final. The Australian opener strikes at over 160 in T20Is and over 170 in the IPL. He previously represented RCB but made a move to SRH in 2024 that saw him score 567 runs in 15 games including one century as well as a joint-fastest fifty by an SRH batter (16 balls). Head formed a potent opening partnership with Abhishek Sharma and is expected to deliver the same again after being retained by SRH for TATA IPL 2025.
