Hailing from South Africa, Matthew Breetzke is an opening wicketkeeper-batter who recently took the headlines to a storm, scripting history by becoming the first batter to score 150 runs on ODI debut. With a strike rate of over 130 in T20s, Breetzke has the ability to give good starts. LSG acquired him up at the 2025 TATA IPL Auction for INR 75 Lakh.
