Abdul PA was bought for his base price of Rs. 20 lakhs by the Rajasthan Royals at the IPL 2023 auction. A left-handed batter, Abdul plays for Kerala. He made his List A and T20 debuts in October-November 2022. What stands out in his short domestic career so far is his strike-rate. He has a strike-rate of 149.31 in 7 T20 innings so far and 96.96 in 3 ODI innings. He would be looking to make the most of the opportunities that come his way as he looks to learn and grow in the biggest cricket league in the world.
