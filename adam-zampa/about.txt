A 28-year-old leg-spinner from New South Wales, Adam Zampa got his first taste of the IPL when he joined the Rising Pune Supergiants in IPL 2016. The leg-spinner was chosen in Australia’s squad for the U-19 World Cup in 2010 and made his first-class debut in November 2012. He possesses impressive control and variety and has also shown leadership skills as captain of Sutherland in Sydney grade competition. Zampa was picked as a replacement for countryman Kane Richardson ahead of the 2020 edition of the league.
