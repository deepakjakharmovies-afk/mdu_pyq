A tall right-arm fast bowler, Prasidh Krishna is known for his raw pace and ability to extract extra bounce. He made an immediate impact in international cricket with a four-wicket haul on his ODI debut against England in 2021. His performances for Karnataka and Kolkata Knight Riders (2018-2021) helped him secure a massive INR 10 Crore deal with Rajasthan Royals in IPL 2022, where he took 19 wickets to guide them to the final. Prasidh was snapped up by Gujarat Titans for INR 9.50 Crore ahead of TATA IPL 2025.
