A promising all-rounder from Haryana, Nishant Sindhu is known for his classy left-handed batting and slow left-arm orthodox bowling. He was a key part of India’s U19 World Cup 2022-winning squad and has made a swift rise in domestic cricket. After spending two seasons with Chennai Super Kings (2023-2024) and learning from legends like MS Dhoni and Ravindra Jadeja, Sindhu will now be part of Gujarat Titans, who signed him for INR 30 Lakh at the 2025 TATA IPL Auction.
