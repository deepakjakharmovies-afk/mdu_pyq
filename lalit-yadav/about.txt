Delhi Capitals bought all-rounder Lalit Yadav for INR 20 Lakh during the IPL Player Auction and have retained him for the IPL 2021. The 24-year-old, who has thus far played 12 First Class matches, put in some impressive all-round performances for Delhi in the latest edition of the Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy and Vijay Hazare Trophy and his IPL side will be wanting him to showcase his skills in the IPL 2021.
