A left-arm spinner in the classical mould who relies on guile and flight to deceive batsmen, Shahbaz Nadeem has been a consistent performer for his state side, Jharkhand at the first-class level. He was transferred for a price of Rs. 3.2 crores from the Delhi Capitals to the Sunrisers Hyderabad side for the 2019 season. Nadeem would look to scale up his performance in the upcoming event and take SRH to higher horizons.
