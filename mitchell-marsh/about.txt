An all-rounder with the ability to hit the big ones while also coming in handy with the ball, Mitchell Marsh, who is also Australia’s T20I captain, has been a regular at the IPL, having played for Deccan Chargers, Pune Warriors India, Rising Pune Supergiant, Sunrisers Hyderabad and Delhi Capitals. Having represented Australia in over 200 matches across all three formats, Marsh was instrumental in Australia’s title-winning run in the T20 World Cup 2021, with a POTM performance in the final. Lucknow Super Giants picked him up for INR 3.4 Crore at the 2025 TATA IPL Auction.
