Hailing from Kerala, Vishnu Vinod is a dynamic wicket-keeper batter who has the ability to hit the long ball. A standout talent in Kerala’s domestic circuit, Vinod made his mark, leading the run charts for his state in the 2019-20 Vijay Hazare Trophy with 508 runs in 8 matches, including three centuries, at an average of 63.50. He made his first-class debut for Kerala in the 2016-17 Ranji Trophy where he showcased his aggressive batting flair early on. Vishnu Vinod has played for Royal Challengers Bengaluru (2017), Delhi Capitals (2021), Sunrisers Hyderabad (2022), and Mumbai Indians (2023-2024). Punjab Kings secured him for INR 95 lakh at the TATA IPL 2025 Auction after a bidding war with Kolkata Knight Riders and Mumbai Indians, a testament to his growing reputation. Vinod now joins PBKS as a vital asset, bringing his explosive batting and reliable glovework to the fore. The franchise will lean on his firepower and finishing ability as they chase their elusive first IPL title in 2025.
