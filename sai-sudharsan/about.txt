A rising left-handed batter from Tamil Nadu, Sai Sudharsan has been a revelation in Indian cricket. He made his international debut in December 2023 and has been a consistent performer in domestic tournaments like the Ranji Trophy, Vijay Hazare Trophy, and Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy. Sudharsan played a key role in GT’s IPL 2022 title win and later showcased his immense potential with a 47-ball 96 in the IPL 2023 final against CSK. His IPL 2024 campaign saw him become the fastest Indian to reach 1000 IPL runs (25 innings) during his century against CSK. Given his meteoric rise, Gujarat Titans retained him ahead of TATA IPL 2025.
