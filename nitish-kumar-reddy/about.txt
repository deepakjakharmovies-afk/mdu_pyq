Nitish Kumar Reddy, a young and dynamic all-rounder from Andhra Pradesh, has already built a remarkable career for himself at an early age. A middle-order batter and a medium pacer, he first grabbed attention with a stunning performance in the 2017-18 Vijay Merchant Trophy, where he started with a triple-century for Andhra, followed by a 441 against Nagaland. Nitish's impressive domestic form earned him a Test debut for India in Australia, where he made an instant impact by scoring a century at the Melbourne Cricket Ground (MCG). His all-round abilities have made him a valuable asset, and he has been a part of Sunrisers Hyderabad since IPL 2023. Recognizing his potential, SRH has retained him for the TATA IPL 2025.
