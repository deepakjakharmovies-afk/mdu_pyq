A two-time ODI World Cup winner and a T20 World Cup champion, Mitchell Starc is one of the most fearsome left-arm pacers in the game. He was sold for a record price of INR 24.75 Crore in IPL 2024 and turned out to be instrumental in KKR’s final victory, delivering a Player of the Match performance against SRH. Recognizing his ability to dominate in high-pressure games, Delhi Capitals spent INR 11.75 crore to bring him in for TATA IPL 2025.
