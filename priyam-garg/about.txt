Captain of the India U19 2020 World Cup, young Priyam Garg led the team to the finals of the tournament. Known to be a gutsy batsman springing up valuable performances in India’s U19 campaign, he was bought by the Sunrisers Hyderabad for 1.9 crores. He played 14 games in his debut IPL season in 2020 and scored one half century. An electric fielder and a handy batsman, Garg will be looking to make the most of this IPL season.
